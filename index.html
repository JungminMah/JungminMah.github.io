About Me.

I am a mechanical engineer with a focus on creating innovative medical devices that bridge engineering and healthcare. My experience includes designing advanced dental implants at Osstem Implant, where I developed user-centric solutions to improve surgical outcomes, and conducting interdisciplinary research at the Korea Institute of Science and Technology. I thrive on tackling complex challenges and transforming ideas into practical solutions that enhance lives.

I am excited to advance wearable medical technologies that offer personalized care and improve daily living. With a strong foundation in engineering and a vision for the future of healthcare, I am committed to making a meaningful impact in the field.
